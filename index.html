Characters Weapons Worlds

Main Characters

  • Kirito - The Black Swordsman and main protagonist.
  • Asuna - The Strong-Willed Vice-Commander of the Knights of the Blood.
  • Leafa - Kirito’s cousin and skilled Sylph warrior.
  • Sinon - A sharpshooter known for her calm demeanor.

Famous Weapons

  • Elucidator - Kirito’s signature black sword.
  • Lambent Light - Asuna’s elegant rapier.
  • Heavensworth - A powerful longsword.
  • Hecate II - Sinon’s sniper rifle.

Virtual Worlds

  • Aincrad - The floating castle world of Sword Art Online.
  • Alfheim Online (ALO) - The magical fairy world.
  • Gun Gale Online (GGO) - A futuristic, gun-based battle arena.
  • Underworld - The advanced virtual reality world from Alicization.
