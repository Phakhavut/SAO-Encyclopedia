Main Characters

  • Kirito (Kazuto Kirigaya) - The Black Swordsman and the main protagonist known for his incredible skill and determination.
  • Asuna (Asuna Yuuki) - Vice-commander of the Knights of the Blood and Kirito’s love interest, a powerful swordswoman.
  • Leafa (Suguha Kirigaya) - Kirito’s cousin and a skilled Sylph player in Alfheim Online.
  • Sinon (Shino Asada) - A sharpshooter known for her cool-headedness and tragic backstory.
  • Klein (Ryoutarou Tsuboi) - Kirito’s close friend and guild leader of Fuurinkazan.
  • Silica (Keiko Ayano) - A beast tamer with her pet dragon Pina.

Notable Villains

  • Kayaba Akihiko - Creator of Sword Art Online and first major antagonist.
  • Heathcliff (Akihiko Kayaba) - The in-game avatar of Kayaba, the ruthless leader of the Knights of the Blood.
  • PoH (Prince of Hell) - Boss of the 75th floor of Aincrad.
  • Death Gun - The deadly assassin in Gun Gale Online.
  • Administrator (Subtilizer) - Main antagonist in Alfheim Online.
  • Eugeo - Important ally turned antagonist in Alicization (complex role).

Famous Weapons

  • Elucidator - Kirito’s signature black sword.
  • Lambent Light - Asuna’s elegant rapier.
  • Dark Repulsor - Kirito’s blue energy blade.
  • Hecate II - Sinon’s sniper rifle.
  • Artemis Bow - Used by Leafa in Alfheim Online.

Skills & Abilities

  • Dual Wielding - Kirito’s unique ability to wield two swords at once.
  • Flash Dash - High-speed movement skill used in Aincrad.
  • Sniping - Sinon’s expert marksmanship skill in Gun Gale Online.
  • Flight - Common skill in Alfheim Online allowing players to fly.
  • System Commands - Special commands like /respawn or /logout in various VRMMORPGs.

Virtual Worlds

  • Aincrad - The iconic floating castle world where the first SAO game takes place.
  • Alfheim Online (ALO) - A magical world with fairy races and flight.
  • Gun Gale Online (GGO) - A futuristic, gun-based battle arena world.
  • Underworld - The advanced virtual world explored in the Alicization arc.
  • Ordinal Scale - Augmented reality world featured in the movie.

Major Story Arcs

  • Aincrad Arc - The first and most iconic arc where players are trapped inside SAO.
  • Fairy Dance Arc - Set in Alfheim Online, focusing on rescuing Asuna.
  • Phantom Bullet Arc - Takes place in Gun Gale Online, dealing with Death Gun.
  • Mother's Rosario Arc - Emotional story about Asuna and a terminally ill player.
  • Alicization Arc - A long arc exploring artificial intelligence and virtual reality ethics.
