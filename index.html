Virtual Worlds

  • Aincrad

    A floating steel castle with 100 floors where the original SAO death game takes place.

  • Alfheim Online (ALO)

    A fantasy world with flying mechanics and different fairy races, focusing on magic and exploration.

  • Gun Gale Online (GGO)

    A gun-based VRMMO world where players battle with firearms in tournaments and PvP combat.

  • Underworld

    A highly advanced simulation for AI development, filled with medieval settings and deep philosophical themes.

Story Arcs

  • Aincrad Arc

    The original survival arc where players are trapped in SAO and must fight to clear all 100 floors.

  • Fairy Dance Arc

    Kirito enters ALO to rescue Asuna, encountering new allies and enemies in a magical setting.

  • Phantom Bullet Arc

    Kirito investigates mysterious deaths in GGO and meets Sinon, a skilled sniper with a painful past.

  • Alicization Arc

    Kirito wakes in the Underworld, a world where time flows differently, and faces his toughest challenges yet.
