Main Characters

  • Kirito (Kazuto Kirigaya)

    The Black Swordsman, known for his skill in dual-wielding and solo play, Kirito is the protagonist who fights to clear Aincrad and protect his friends.

  • Asuna Yuuki

    Vice-commander of the Knights of the Blood, Asuna is a fierce and skilled swordswoman who fights alongside Kirito and is his beloved partner.

  • Leafa (Suguha Kirigaya)

    Kirito’s cousin and expert Sylph player in Alfheim Online, she is skilled in magic and flight combat.

  • Sinon (Shino Asada)

    Expert sniper in Gun Gale Online, Sinon overcomes her traumatic past and becomes a trusted ally of Kirito.

  • Klein (Ryoutarou Tsuboi)

    Kirito’s loyal friend and leader of the Fuurinkazan guild, Klein is known for his bravery and camaraderie.

  • Silica (Keiko Ayano)

    A beast tamer who travels with her pet dragon Pina, Silica is cheerful and brave despite her young age.

Notable Villains

  • Kayaba Akihiko

    Creator of Sword Art Online, Kayaba trapped thousands of players in the game, forcing them to clear the levels or face death in real life.

  • Zekken (Death Gun)

    An infamous assassin in Gun Gale Online, capable of killing players both in-game and in real life.

  • Argo

    An information broker with dubious motives, Argo trades valuable intel and often operates in morally grey zones.

Iconic Weapons

  • Elucidator

    Kirito’s primary sword in Aincrad, a sleek black blade known for its sharpness and power.

  • Lambent Light

    Asuna’s elegant rapier, prized for speed and precision in battle.

  • PSG1 Sniper Rifle

    Sinon's signature sniper rifle in Gun Gale Online, famous for deadly accuracy.

Skills & Abilities

  • Dual Wielding

    A rare skill possessed by Kirito allowing him to wield two swords simultaneously for rapid and powerful attacks.

  • Healing Magic

    Used by various characters like Lisbeth and Silica to support their teammates during combat.

  • Sniping

    A high precision skill that Sinon masters in Gun Gale Online to eliminate targets from a distance.

Virtual Worlds

  • Aincrad

    A floating castle with 100 floors, setting of the original Sword Art Online game.

  • Alfheim Online (ALO)

    A magical world filled with fairies and flying combat, featured prominently in the Fairy Dance arc.

  • Gun Gale Online (GGO)

    A futuristic shooting game where players use firearms and tactics, key in the Phantom Bullet arc.

  • Underworld

    An advanced virtual world featured in the Alicization arc, with complex AI and ethical dilemmas.

Story Arcs

  • Aincrad Arc

    The original survival game where Kirito and thousands of others are trapped, fighting to clear 100 floors.

  • Fairy Dance Arc

    Kirito enters Alfheim Online to rescue Asuna from captivity within the virtual world.

  • Phantom Bullet Arc

    Kirito investigates mysterious deaths linked to Gun Gale Online’s Death Gun.

  • Alicization Arc

    A long arc exploring artificial intelligence and virtual reality ethics in the Underworld.
